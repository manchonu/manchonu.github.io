Macau (traditional Chinese: 澳門; simplified Chinese: 澳门), also spelled Macao (/məˈkaʊ/), is along with Hong Kong, one of the two special administrative regions of the People's Republic of China. It lies on the western side of the Pearl River Delta, bordering Guangdong province to the north and facing the South China Sea to the east and south. When Dr.U grew up...
Dr. U (or Kevin) grew up in Macau. He studied in The Workers' Children High School (Macau) and graduated with the degree of science in 2002. As a youth, Dr. U enjoyed athletics. In junior high school he was a member of the school's shotput team. In senior high school he switched to Karate, then becoming a member of the Macau national team. Dr. U represented Macau to participate in the 5th Asian Junior Karate Competition in year 2000, and the 2nd World Junior Karate Competition in 2001. In 2002, Dr. U left the team to attend college at Shanghai University.

At Shanghai University, Dr. U majored in computer science, receiving his Bachelor of Engineering degree in 2006. While there he was selected to take part in the research project in the Computer Systems Research Laboratory of the Computer Science department. Given on this opportunity, Dr. U worked very hard and his final year project received an A grade, an accomplishment shared by only 3% of nearly 300 students in the whole department. Furthermore, Dr. U's work was appraised by the University as "The Excellent Thesis of Shanghai University" and was also published in both national and interntional conferences. In 2005, Dr. U was selected by Shanghai University as one of the five Elite Student Representatives participated in the first exchange program between Shanghai University and Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich. In the exchange program, he worked at the headquater of Webasto AG. in Munich (Germany) as an IT intern. His job role was to provide software development to the company.

Upon graduation from Shanghai University, Dr. U decided to return back to Macau to begin his professional career. He worked at Cable & Wireless Communications (Macau Branch: CTM) for about two years, beginning in the position of Assistant Analyst Programmer and after 14 months promoted to the Senior level. When Dr. U was in CTM, he learned a lot about large-scale software systems, gained deeper sight in the telecom provisioning areas, and became sophisticated in Linux/UNIX systems and Oracle database. He has also learned about how security equipments (i.e. IPS, VPN, Firewall, etc.) work in a gaint industry, how to perform security incident response, investigation, and compliance review. He was also responsible for managing all Disaster Recovery Drills in the company. In August 2008, Dr. U left CTM in order to pursue his Ph.D. in computer science, having been granted a full scholarship to attend The University of Georgia (UGA) in USA.

What has Dr. U been doing lately?

At UGA, Dr. U's (or Kevin's) research focus was Machine Learning, he also interested in NLP, Evolutionary Computation, Artificial Intelligence, Computer & Network Security, and Computational Biology. His major advisor is Professor Khaled Rasheed who is the director of the Evolutionary Computation & Machine Learning (ECML) Lab at UGA. Detailed descriptions of Dr. U's research projects are available at here. Besides research, Dr. U also developed the Web-based Noah's Arkive, which is a web-based system that allows pathologists to search for desired pathological images. The system is well recognized in the community of veterinary medicine, their customers are not only fellows from academia but also researchers from industry. In the summer of 2012, Dr. U worked at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center as an research intern, conducted research in the Cloud Performance and Availability Management area. Implemented solutions with Java and apply various Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing techniques to automatically detect and consolidate ticket data that caused by the same root cause to effectively reduce corresponding management costs. In August 2013, Dr. U officially graduated from UGA with his PhD in computer scince.

Upon graduation from UGA, Dr. U joined Think Big Analytics (Mountain View, CA) as a Senior Data Engineer to design and implement large-scale/big data processing solutions for corporations utilizing various technologies (Hadoop, Hive, Pig, HBase, Oozie, Java, Shell script, Python, Machine Learning, NLP, etc.). Involved projects covered a wide variety of industries such as technology, finance, and advertising (real-time bidding, programmetic, etc.).

In 2014, Dr. U got some opportunities from Goldman Sachs, Yahoo!, and Las Vegas Sands Corp.; The offer from Goldman Sachs was the most attractive, however, given the bond between Dr. U and Macau; He en up joining Las Vegas Sands Corp. as a Seniior Manager of Data Science to help building out the Data Science team. Dr. U was the first Data Scientist at Las Vegas Sands Corp., then he built out the team across multiple markets (Las Vegas, Singapore, and Macau), managed a team of Statistics, Data/Text mining, Machine Learning, and Economics Experts. Dr. U's main focus at Las Vegas Sands Corp. was advanced analytics, data science, data strategy & best practices, and corresponding technology initiatives. Dr. U was the Global Director of Data Science when he left Las Vegas Sands Corp. in 2017.

In 2017, Dr. U was approached by multiple opportunities, by April 2017 he recevied offers from 20th Century Fox at LA as VP of Data Science & Engineering, Credit Sesame at SF as VP of Data Science, Cigna at NYC as Head of Data Science, Carnival Cruise Line at Miami as Sr. Director/Head of Marketing Analytics & Data Science, and InsurTech Big Data startup at Beijing as CTO. Finally, the CMO (Kathy Mayor) at Carnival Cruise Line end up convinced Dr. U joining them by her ambitious plans and visions. It was the second time that Dr. U giving up more money as well as his favorite city (NYC) by chosing something he feels interested. Well... Life is too short to do the things you don't love doing! In Carnival Cruise Line, Dr. U is leading the marketing analytics and data science team as a centralized service unit under the marketing department. Oversees analytics & data science practice, data strategy, and corresponding technology initiatives (Big Data, Data Lake, DMP, CDP, MarTech, MediaTech, etc.). Responsible for maximizing the use of technologies, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning to drive business growth. Reporting directly to the Chief Marketing Officer and work closely with the office of the Chief Information Officer to design and implement data and system architecture as well as corresponding platforms to enable advanced data science and engineering capabilities.
